White Collar Legal LLP

We are a boutique Corporate and IP Law firm based in Pune, with offices in Mumbai and Aurangabad.

We are comprised of creative Chartered Accountants, cheerful Company Secretaries and Learned Lawyers.

What We Do

  • Formation and Closure of Companies/LLPs
  • Business Setup Services
  • Registration,Certification and Licensing
  • RBI and FEMA Compliance
  • Secretarial,Legal and Due Diligence Audits
  • Corporate Law and Taxation Advisory
  • Verification and Searches
  • Maintenance Assistance and MCA Seervices
  • Drafting Compliance
  • Strategic Planning and Strategy Development
  • SEBI and Listing
  • Statutory Certification
  • Legal Process Outsourcing
  • Licenses and Liasioning with Governmental Authorities
  • Employment/Labour Law Compliances
  • Arbitration matters
  • Infrastructure and Project Finance arrangements
  • NGO,Trust,Society,Not for Profit Formation
  • Investment Documentation
  • Right to Information Requests
  • Property Matters
  • Legal Advice and Support
